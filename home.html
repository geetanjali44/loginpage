Santarpana Details

🙏 What is Santarpana?

Santarpana means serving food/prasadam to devotees with devotion during Ganesh Utsav celebrations.

🍛 Purpose

Donations are used for prasadam, annadanam, pooja needs, and serving devotees.

📍 Location

Sri Varasidhi Vinayaka Mandapam, Tanuku, Andhra Pradesh.
